🍴 Nutrition Basics

What is the best diet for my dog?

A balanced mix of high-quality protein, essential fats, and complex carbohydrates tailored to their breed and activity level.

Side-by-side comparison of artisanal grain-free nuggets and whole grain-inclusive pet food on a rustic wooden board
📊

Should I choose grain-free or grain-inclusive food?

It depends on your dog's sensitivities. Many dogs thrive on ancient grains, while others require grain-free alternatives.

🕐

How often should I feed my dog?

Adorable golden retriever puppy sitting patiently in front of an empty bowl in a bright sunlit morning room

Puppies require 3–4 small meals, while adult dogs typically do best with two consistent feedings per day.

Lifestyle

How much exercise does my dog need?

Daily exercise is vital for metabolic health. Most breeds need at least 45–60 minutes of active engagement.

Energetic border collie running through a lush green field at sunset with golden light catching its fur
Senior labrador with senior food

How should I adjust diet for senior dogs?

Focus on high digestibility and joint support nutrients like Glucosamine as their metabolism slows down.

Hands preparing homemade dog food

Can I give homemade food to my dog?

Yes, but it must be nutritionally complete. Always consult with a veterinary nutritionist before switching to home-cooked.
