Paws for a Cause 🐕

Every day, thousands of stray dogs in India go hungry. At Doggy Diet India, we believe every dog — pet or stray — deserves proper nutrition and care.

Our NGO wing, Paws for a Cause, works with local volunteers to feed, vaccinate, and rehabilitate stray dogs across Punjab, Haryana, and Delhi NCR.

Your donation — however small — makes a real difference. ₹100 feeds a stray dog for a week. ₹500 covers a vaccination. ₹1000 supports a rescue operation.
