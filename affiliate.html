Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the Partner Program?

We welcome registered pet shops, veterinary clinics, certified resellers, and regional distributors across India. B2B verification is mandatory.

What are the expected profit margins?

Partners typically enjoy 25-40% margins depending on order volume and SKU selection. Detailed tier pricing is provided after approval.

How fast is the delivery?

Metro cities usually receive shipments within 48-72 hours. Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities may take up to 4-5 working days.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Yes, we have flexible MOQ tiers starting from as low as 50 units per order. Details are shared during onboarding.
