Our Story

Doggy Diet India was born out of love for dogs and frustration with low-quality pet food in the Indian market. We started in 2022 with one simple mission — give Indian dogs the nutrition they truly deserve.

Based in Zirakpur, Mohali, we work with veterinary nutritionists to craft every recipe. No fillers, no artificial preservatives — just real, wholesome ingredients your dog will love.

Today, we're proud to serve 50,000+ happy dogs across India! 🎉
