Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my delivery take?

Most orders reach metro cities within 3-4 days. Tier 2 and rural areas may take up to 7 business days.

Can I return an unopened bag?

To maintain our safety standards and prevent cross-contamination, we do not accept any returns once the product is shipped.

What if the seal is broken?

Please do not accept the delivery. If already accepted, email us clear photos within 24 hours for a replacement.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, Doggy Diet India ships exclusively within India to ensure our fresh batches reach pets in peak condition.
